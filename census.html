Data Science and the census (for high school)

These are videos and brief assignments meant to serve as a "module" on the US Census, and how its count of the population is used for drawing districts for the US Census. This will be appropriate for high schoolers, with or without programming experience; the last project will have an optional programming component which students with a bit of Python experience can explore.

For an introduction to what this module is and how you might use it, please see our Overview of the module for instructors (PDF). For a brief slide overview, you can see this presentation (PDF)
