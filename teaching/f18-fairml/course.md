This course serves as an introduction to the study of fairness in machine learning.

Topics covered include:

  -   numerous examples of "unfair" ML behavior
  -   theoretical background for machine learning
  -   the ML "pipeline": more than just one-shot classification
  -   sources of unfairness
  -   the milieu of fairness definitions
  -   fairness interventions
  -   long-term consequences of fairness interventions
  -   limitations of observational data
